Align Supply team leads on baseline AI adoption status and commit to 1-3 high-value experiments for the next sprint.

- AI Lead (facilitator)
- Engineering Managers (Supply domains)
- Tech Leads
- PM representatives
- QA representative

### 0:16-0:24 - Choose Experiments (8 min)

### 0:24-0:28 - Risks and Guardrails (4 min)

### 0:28-0:30 - Commitments and Next Checkpoint (2 min)
